# Reference Search Pack

## Goal

Provide a curated search set for `advanced` reference locking and page-level image or style discovery.

This pack is not a generic list of design sites.
It separates:

- visual-direction sources
- real-deck structure sources
- Chinese PPT and office-oriented sources

## Use Order

When browsing is available, use the search set in this order:

1. lock the style direction first
2. search high-signal global presentation references
3. search structure-oriented deck sources if the story rhythm still feels unclear
4. search Chinese PPT sources when the target audience, business context, or local visual language matters

## Global High-Signal Sources

### Behance

- URL: `https://www.behance.net/search/projects/presentation`
- Best for: locking overall visual direction
- Borrow from it:
  - typography hierarchy
  - spacing rhythm
  - cover language
  - image treatment
- Do not over-trust:
  - purely decorative shots that do not read like real slides

### Dribbble

- URL: `https://dribbble.com/search/presentation`
- Best for: local visual language
- Borrow from it:
  - cover mood
  - section-page treatment
  - title treatment
  - page furniture
- Do not over-trust:
  - one-shot concept art that cannot scale into a full deck

### SlideShare

- URL: `https://www.slideshare.net/`
- Best for: real-world deck rhythm and page sequencing
- Borrow from it:
  - how many pages a story needs
  - how arguments unfold across slides
  - how data-heavy decks pace themselves
- Do not over-trust:
  - outdated corporate styling

### Pitch

- URL: `https://pitch.com/`
- Best for: modern SaaS, product, sales, and board-deck patterns
- Helpful sections:
  - `https://pitch.com/use-cases/pitch-decks`
  - `https://pitch.com/use-cases/board-decks`
  - `https://pitch.com/academy`
- Borrow from it:
  - deck structure
  - modern business layout grammar
  - lightweight, clean product storytelling

## Chinese PPT And Design Sources

### 优品PPT

- URL: `https://www.ypppt.com/`
- Best for: broad Chinese PPT template coverage
- Good for:
  - local topic phrasing
  - common Chinese business and education slide patterns
  - background and chart asset references
- Use carefully:
  - treat it as a signal pool, not as the final aesthetic ceiling

### OfficePLUS

- URL: `https://www.officeplus.cn/`
- Best for: office-friendly Chinese PPT references
- Good for:
  - mainstream workplace decks
  - safe business layout patterns
  - practical office scenarios such as reports, teaching, and summaries
- Use carefully:
  - results may be too safe when the deck needs a stronger visual signature

### 稻壳儿 / Docer

- URL: `https://www.docer.com/`
- Best for: WPS-friendly Chinese template and asset discovery
- Good for:
  - local workplace scenarios
  - education, summary, and report-oriented patterns
  - common domestic office visual language
- Use carefully:
  - many results are utility-first rather than design-first

### 51PPT模板网

- URL: `https://www.51pptmoban.com/`
- Best for: Chinese PPT template breadth and category coverage
- Good for:
  - finding how local categories are commonly framed
  - quick scans of Chinese style conventions
  - supplementary assets and chart ideas
- Use carefully:
  - quality varies, so filter aggressively

### 站酷 ZCOOL

- URL: `https://www.zcool.com.cn/`
- Best for: Chinese designer-made PPT and Keynote references
- Good for:
  - stronger visual references than generic template sites
  - cover ideas
  - Chinese presentation styling with higher design signal
- Suggested use:
  - search `PPT/Keynote`
  - search `PPT设计`
  - search `PPT封面`

### iSlide

- URL: `https://www.islide.cc/`
- Best for: Chinese commercial presentation ecosystem context
- Good for:
  - understanding common Chinese presentation assets and efficiency patterns
  - locating office-oriented visual modules and presentation helpers
- Use carefully:
  - this is more of an ecosystem and asset context source than a pure gallery

## Query Construction

Build queries from four parts:

1. style word
2. deck type
3. topic word
4. optional filter word

Examples:

- `editorial presentation design ai workflow`
- `minimal keynote product strategy slides`
- `clean consulting slide design market analysis`
- `科技风 PPT 设计 产品发布`
- `杂志风 PPT 封面 站酷`
- `商务汇报 PPT 模板 OfficePLUS`

Helpful filter words:

- `presentation design`
- `slide design`
- `keynote`
- `pitch deck`
- `board deck`
- `PPT设计`
- `PPT/Keynote`
- `PPT封面`

## Selection Rules

Pick references that help the workflow answer:

- what should the title hierarchy feel like
- how dense should a page be
- how should imagery be cropped or framed
- what page furniture is useful
- how should cover, section, and content slides differ

Do not pick references just because they look flashy.

Reject references that are:

- too poster-like
- too webpage-like
- too decorative to scale
- too generic to teach anything

## Practical Routing

- Use `Behance + Dribbble` to lock visual direction.
- Use `SlideShare + Pitch` to understand real deck pacing.
- Use `优品PPT + OfficePLUS + Docer + 51PPT模板网` to understand Chinese office and education deck conventions.
- Use `站酷` when the deck needs stronger Chinese visual signal than template sites can provide.
